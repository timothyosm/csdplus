Ticket/PR search

You have the ability to search tickets through CSD+. To search for a ticket enter the ticket number and click the corresponding ticket. For Problem tickets enter the description of the problem and click 'SEARCH PR'

Dynamic Replies

Dynamic replies scrape data from the CSD to populate replies to customers, the replies in the CSD are education centric. If you want one added to the system email Timothy.osmond@civica.com.au

  1. Navigate to a reply Screen, "Add Customer Note" for example"
  2. Click the reply you want to use, this will send it to your clipboard
  3. Paste the reply into the text area

Forms

To access a form simply click to corresponding link for the required process.
    Fill out the form and click submit, this will populate the data on the right hand side. Now just copy and paste this into your ticket body.

If a required field is left blank, it will be highlighted in red when you hit submit.
